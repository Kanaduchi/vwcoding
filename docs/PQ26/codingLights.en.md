* Abbiegelicht links – left turn light CORNER
* Abbiegelicht rechts – right turn light CORNER

* Nebellicht rechts – right fog light  
* Parking light – parking lights

* Parking light right – right parking lights
* ComingHome LeavingHome – illumination of leaving home and meeting
* Abblendlicht links – low beam left headlight
* Abblendlicht rechts – low beam right headlight
* Fernlicht links – with far left fire
* Fernlicht rechts – with far right fire
* Lichthupe generell – turns on when blinking distant
* Left high beam – left high beam
* Right high beam – right high beam
* Headlamp flasher – distant blinking
* Tagfahrlicht – DRL
* Daytime running lights – DRL
* Blinken links Hellphase – when the left turn signal lights up
* Blinken links Dunkelphase – when the left turn signal is dimmed
* Blinken rechts Hellphase – when the right turn signal lights up
* Blinken rechts Dunkelphase – when the right turn signal goes off
* Blinken links aktiv (beide Phase) – constantly when the left turn signal is on
* Blinken rechts aktiv (beide Phase) - constantly when the right turn signal is working
* Warnblinktaster – light alarm
* SafeLED – security alarm operation
* Brake light – brake lights
* Nebelschlusslicht – when turning on the rear PTF
* Rueckfahrlicht – reversing light

* Parklicht rechts – lights up in park mode. fire right (light off)
* Heckdeckel offen – lights up when the trunk is opened
* Heckdeckel geschlossen – lights up when the trunk is closed
